    What the 12th group learned from studying Human computer interaction


    Knowledge:

    • Learn more new knowledge (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Template, Bootstrap).
    • Improve teamwork. Know how to design a website.
    • Apply theory learned to interface design.

    Ability to work in groups:

    • Because for the first time, group of 12 worked together as a group, so they were still confused, the effectiveness of group work was not achieved at first, and there were different opinions.
    • But the group of 12 has a common goal of completing the most complete and optimal website.
    • Therefore, the whole team clearly understood the goals and committed to strive, and were responsible for each member's assigned work results when participating in the website making, the group's activities became smoother.
    • Starting to work on an interface that the team has chosen for a long time, the members are also interested in the assigned work. Thanks to the openness, expressing thoughts and learning from each other during group work, each working session is like a learning session for each member of the group.
    • After a period of working together, the group of 12 had different ideas that were creative, new and completed the work assigned by the team leader, the website was completed the best.
    • All this is thanks to the reasonable division of work of the team leader and listening to the team members. The members have received and completed the work in the best way.

    Accumulate skills:

    • Through her study, practice and exchange with classmates.
    • From there, the lessons can be drawn and the group's shortcomings can be overcome.
    • Acquire her long-term work experience and skills to prepare well for studying, training and getting a good foundation.
    • Shape her way of working as well as her meticulousness and meticulousness, thereby learning her way of working and living.
    • She once shared: "She works a lot, but in her person she always keeps comfort and moreover, gets sympathy from many people". Actually, the group of 12 really wanted to get those things from her.

    Thank you:

    • First of all, group 12 would like to express their sincere and deep thanks to the subject lecturer - Ms. Nga for teaching and imparting useful knowledge during the past study period.
    • During her time in her Robotic Communication class, group 12 gained a lot of new knowledge, a more effective and serious team spirit.
    • Although my team has tried their best, it will certainly be difficult to avoid shortcomings and many inaccuracies. Please consider and give suggestions to improve the website of group 12.
    • Group 12 would like to sincerely thank her, wish her a lot of health and lots of joy in life.
